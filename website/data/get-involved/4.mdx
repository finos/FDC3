## Are you using FDC3?

If you are an existing user of the FDC3 Standard, we would love to hear from you: just email the [FDC3 General List](mailto:fdc3@finos.org) with details about how you are using it. 

We'd also like to list you as a user on our [Community page](https://fdc3.finos.org/community), you can directly [send a pull request](https://github.com/finos/FDC3/edit/main/website/data/community.json) or [contact us](mailto:fdc3-private@finos.org) (particularly if you need help with legal evaluation of your logo).
