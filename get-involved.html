Get Involved in FDC3

The FDC3 Standard is the result of the work of a dedicated group of contributors from both FINOS members and non-member contributing organizations alike. Ready to get involved in our community? Here are some ways to get started:

Collaborate with the Community

Most FDC3 collaboration takes place in the FDC3 GitHub repository, where you can raise issues, submit pull requests and view meeting minutes.

Learn more about the Standard

Join the General List (and check out the archives) to stay up to date with the project. You can also attend the quarterly FDC3 General Meeting: you can find meeting details in the FINOS Project Calendar.

Take the free FDC3 Training.

Participate in the Standard's ideation

The Standard Working Group meets once a month to shape the next version of the Standard: you can find meeting details in the FINOS Project Calendar. If you'd like to formally enroll as a voting Standard participant, please follow these instructions.

Are you using FDC3?

If you are an existing user of the FDC3 Standard, we would love to hear from you: just email the FDC3 General List with details about how you are using it. If you'd like to be listed as a user on our homepage, you can directly send a pull request or, contact us if you need help with legal evaluation of your logo.
